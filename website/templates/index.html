Money Pit

The stock market simulator where the goal is to lose.

Compete against others and trade using realtime market prices to loose your money as fast as possible!

Initially you will start with $1 million and you will attempt to get down to 5% of your starting capital. The reason for this is that you will almost never get all of the way to 0 because it would require the company to go bankrupt which doesn't happen often enough.
